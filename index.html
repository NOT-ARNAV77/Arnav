Gaming

What actually is a Video Game?

A video game, also known as computer game is an electronic game that can be controlled by various different input methods.

A video game involves interaction with user interface or a physical input device To generate visual feedback from the display device.
The visual feedback provided by the game is most commonly shown as a video, hence the name Video games. The feedback is shown on some format of a screen

Types of devices to play on

Video games are played on a ton of platforms like, PC, Mobile Phones, Handheld consoles, Arcade Machines and also Consoles like Playstation and Xbox

There are a lot of different input and output devices for video games, the output devices are mostly monitors or TV screen but can also be the screen of handhelds like mobile phones or a Nintendo Switch.
There is a huge variety of input devices awailable to play video games, most common being Keyboard and Mouse, various different kinds of controllers, handhelds, Mobile phones, joysticks, or even racing sim setups for cars or aircrafts.

The gaming industry

The Gaming industry is massive and constantly growing at a rapid speed. There are new games being produced, better technological advancements making it easier for game making companies to improve their games and new and better technology also allowing gamers to play higher end games. The gaming industry is widely known with 81 percent of the entire population playing games, casually or professionaly.
